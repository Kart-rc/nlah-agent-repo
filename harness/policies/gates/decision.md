# Decision Gate Checklist

Required for: technical decision records, architecture verdicts, and any
artifact that commits the organization to a course of action. Verify the
artifact provides:

- [ ] The decision stated in one sentence, with an owner and a date
- [ ] Losing options named, with the reason each loses
- [ ] Rationale traces to evidence, not to authority
- [ ] Reversibility classified (one-way vs two-way door), with reasoning
- [ ] Strongest dissent recorded and answered, not omitted
- [ ] Revisit triggers concrete and observable
- [ ] Time-bound: decided at stated confidence rather than deferred without a date
